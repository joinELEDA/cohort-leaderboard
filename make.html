Make a leaderboard

Turn a Google Sheet into a live leaderboard for your cohort. No account, nothing to install. Founders submit numbers through your Google Form; this page gives you a link to the live board. Never seen one? View the demo.

  1. Copy the template Form and Settings sheet (links and full instructions in the setup guide).
  2. In each spreadsheet: File > Share > Publish to web, pick the tab, choose Comma-separated values (.csv), publish, copy the URL.
