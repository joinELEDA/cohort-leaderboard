Make a leaderboard

A live scoreboard for your cohort. Participants submit numbers through a Google Form; you get a link to put on the projector. Free, no account. Never seen one? View the demo.

Before you fill this in (about 10 minutes in Google):

  1. Copy the template form, then in your copy click Publish (top right), then Responses > Link to Sheets to create your responses spreadsheet.
  2. In that responses spreadsheet: File > Share > Publish to web. First dropdown: pick Form Responses 1. Second dropdown: pick Comma-separated values (.csv). Publish, copy the URL.
  3. Fill in the boxes below. Done.
